Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 22 September 2025

We respect your privacy and provide you with the ability to delete your data from our application. There are two ways to request data deletion:

1. Deletion within the Application

You can delete your social media connections directly within the app:

  1. Go to the 'Social Networks' page in your dashboard.
  2. Find the social account you wish to remove.
  3. Click the 'Delete' button next to the account name.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

This will remove your access tokens and any data stored from that specific social media platform.

2. Manual Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated data, please send an email to our support team at salehalkhleefi45@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Please include the following information in your email:

  • Your username or the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request and confirm the deletion of your data within 30 days.

By using our application, you agree to this data deletion policy.
